FAQ

Questions students ask before starting

Is JIGSAW free to use?

Yes — browsing resources, roadmaps, and the community board is completely free. Some individual courses linked from the platform are paid, and that's always labeled clearly before you click through.

How are resources chosen for the catalog?

Every submission is reviewed for relevance, currency, and instructional quality before it's added. Outdated or broken resources are flagged by the community and removed.

Do I need to create an account to browse?

No — resources, coursework, and pathways are all viewable without signing in. An account lets you save progress, post in the community, and track your pathway completion.

Can I suggest a resource that's missing?

Yes — use the contact page's feedback form, or post in the relevant community category. Most suggestions are reviewed within a few days.

Get one useful resource a week

No spam, no daily emails — just a single well-chosen resource or roadmap update every week.
