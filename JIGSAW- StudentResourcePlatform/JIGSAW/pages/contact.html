FAQ

Common questions

How quickly will I get a response?

Most messages are answered within 3–5 working days. Broken-link reports are usually resolved faster since they affect other students too.

Can I request a specific resource be added?

Yes — select "Suggest a Resource" in the form above and include a link plus a short note on why it's worth adding.

Is there a way to report a technical bug on the site itself?

Yes, use the "Report a Broken Link" category for content issues, or mention it's a site bug directly in your message — either routes to our team.
